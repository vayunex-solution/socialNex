MASTER SaaS LAUNCH SOP

The ultimate checklist to launch, scale, and monetize any SaaS product or website without wasting time on useless features.

1

Idea & Market Reality Check (Pre-Code Phase)

Rule: Code likhne se pehle ye clear hona chahiye ki hum kya bana rahe hain aur kiske liye bana rahe hain. No blind coding.

  • The "One" Focus Rule: Am I pausing other distractions?Focus on ONE core product at a time. No shiny object syndrome.
  • Define the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile): Ye product exactly kiske liye hai?e.g., "Not for everyone. It's for local Gym Owners & Real Estate Brokers in Yamunanagar/Tricity who need daily social media posts".
  • The Painkiller Test: Is this solving a real problem (saving time/money), or is it just a "cool" feature?Cool features don't make money.
  • Competitor Bypass Strategy: Hum market giants (Hootsuite, AWS) se direct fight nahi karenge.Humari niche strategy aur 'Unfair Advantage' (like custom AI tools) clear honi chahiye.
2

Frontend & UX Architecture (The Build Phase)

Rule: UI sundar hone se pehle, fast hona chahiye. Speed is the ultimate feature.

  • Mobile-First Check: Is the UI perfectly responsive?80% of your target audience will open this on their phones.
  • Asset Optimization:
    • No PNGs or JPGs. Convert all images to WebP or AVIF.
    • Use SVGs for logos and UI icons.
  • Code Splitting & Lazy Loading: Heavy components (dashboards, charts) aur below-the-fold images lazy-load ho rahe hain?
  • Lighthouse Performance Audit: Google PageSpeed Insights par Desktop aur Mobile dono ka score 90+ hona chahiye before going public.
3

SEO, AEO & GEO (The Discovery Engine)

Rule: Search engines aur AI bots dono ko tera content clearly samajh aana chahiye.

  • Traditional SEO Meta Tags:
    • Unique <title> and <meta description> with 1 primary keyword.
    • Open Graph (OG) Tags set up for Facebook/LinkedIn/WhatsApp link previews.
  • AEO (Answer Engine Optimization): FAQ Schema Markup (JSON-LD) injected for Voice Search (Alexa/Siri) and Google 'People Also Ask'.
  • GEO (Generative Engine Optimization): Proper HTML5 semantic tags (<header>, <main>, <article>, <strong>) for AI bots (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini).
  • Sitemap & Robots.txt:
    • sitemap.xml generated and placed in the public folder.
    • robots.txt created to guide search crawlers.
  • Google Search Console (GSC):
    • Domain verified.
    • Sitemap submitted.
    • Requested forced indexing for the homepage.
4

Security & Legal Compliance (Don't Get Sued or Billed)

Rule: Ek CSE AIML founder hone ke naate, tera backend aur API keys iron-clad hone chahiye.

  • API Rate Limiting: Gemini/OpenAI endpoints par strict rate-limiting lagi hai?e.g., Max 5 poster generations per IP per hour for free users. Stop bot abuse.
  • Environment Variables: All API keys, database URIs, and JWT secrets are strictly in .env files and never exposed to the frontend?
  • CORS Policy: Backend strict CORS policy follow kar raha hai so only vayunexsolution.com domains can request data?
  • Legal Pages (Mandatory for Payment Gateways):
    • Privacy Policy page is live.
    • Terms of Service page is live.
    • Refund/Cancellation Policy is live.
‚ö†Ô∏è Payment Gateway Warning: Razorpay/Stripe tera account reject kar denge agar Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, aur Refund Policy pages live nahi honge. Ye mandatory requirement hai.
5

Analytics & Tracking (Data Over Emotions)

Rule: Jo measure nahi ho sakta, wo grow nahi ho sakta. Feelings pe business nahi chalta.

  • Traffic Tracking: Google Analytics (GA4) or PostHog script injected in the <head>.
  • Conversion Event Tracking: "Sign Up", "Pricing", and "Start Free Trial" buttons are tracked to measure conversion rates.
  • User Behavior (Heatmaps): Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity installed to record where users are clicking and where they are dropping off.
6

Go-To-Market (GTM) & Sales Protocol (Making Money)

Rule: Building is 20%, Selling is 80%. Website live hona launch nahi hota, pehla paisa aana launch hota hai.

  • Zero Ads Policy for V1: Start with organic reach. No burning cash on Google/FB ads until product-market fit is proven.
  • The Hitlist: Excel sheet of 50-100 highly targeted local businesses is ready with contact info.
  • Show, Don't Tell (Demo Strategy): Do not just send links. Physically visit or do a WhatsApp video call to show a live demo.e.g., generating their brand's poster in 5 seconds.
  • Concierge Onboarding: Manually set up the accounts for the first 10 users to ensure they don't churn due to confusion.
  • The Ask: Transition from free trial to paid subscription fearlessly after 7 days.Feedback is nice, but MRR is the goal.
7

Feedback Loop & Retention (Keep Them Paying)

Rule: Naya user lana mehenga hai, purane ko roke rakhna sasta hai.

  • In-App Bug Reporting: Ek simple way for users to report bugs without leaving the dashboard.
  • Feature Request Board: Let users tell you what they actually want to pay for next, instead of guessing.
